Aster Hospital Qusais
Dubai, UAE

Aster Hospital Qatar is a 150 bedded multispecialty specialty hospital. Aster Hospital Qusais is equipped with the latest medical technology, 9 beds in the neonatal intensive care unit, 4 beds for obstetrics, 10 beds for intensive care and emergency services and a pharmacy open around the clock. Aster Hospital Qusais is notably renowned for providing multidisciplinary medical treatment and surgery as well as for its most developed unit of intensive care for newborns in the UAE.

Aster Hospital Qusais is built with ample interior designs, the latest medical technology, and the presence of the best-dedicated health care experts, all for the comfort of its patients.
