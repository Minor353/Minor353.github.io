About us

Eurohealth Systems (EHS), is a Dubai based firm specialized in healthcare and advisory consultancy within the MENA region. It offers complete healthcare management services to operators and investors within the healthcare sector. With over a decade of healthcare experience, EHS is a pioneer in the field of globalizing healthcare practice through a complete spectrum of healthcare consultancy, outsourcing and management services.

Company overview

Founded in 2006, Eurohealth Systems FZ LLC (EHS) have grown to become pioneers in the field of globalizing healthcare practice through a complete spectrum of healthcare consultancy, outsourcing and management services. Operating from Dubai Healthcare City in the UAE, we are strategically positioned to serve clients in both developed and emerging markets, through links with healthcare professionals across the Americas, Europe, Asia and the Middle-East.

To provide the highest quality of professional services that address our clients’ business needs, in order to enhance their operations through streamlining processes, empowering people, improving financial performance and transforming healthcare delivery through technology.

  • In-depth Expertise, Seamless Solutions

    Working in the primary areas of Hospital Consultation, Hospital Development & Management, EHS has expertise in planning, commissioning and operating Primary, Secondary and Tertiary care Facilities. Our team works closely with each client to reduce project and operational costs through value engineering & re-engineering while enhancing efficiency and streamlining internal processes. Our subject matter expertise, practical experience and technological edge enable us to carefully cater to our client’s every need from hospital design-concept to commissioning, consulting, outsourcing and management development.

    Through strategic tie-ups with world class technology providers, we also deliver customized, integrated IT solutions for Hospital and Clinic software to provide an efficient Hospital Information System implementation for paperless medical establishments.

  • Robust Partnerships, Lasting Value

    Poised at the forefront of a fast evolving industry, we understand that reducing costs and complexity of hospital operating systems is crucial for the growth of healthcare companies. In an emerging market where changing customer expectations, market dynamics and regulatory demands are the norm, companies are hard-pressed to deliver greater value and better quality at a lower cost. This is where the ability to evolve and adapt becomes imperative.
    We partner with governments, healthcare providers, institutions, hospitals, and investors, with the aim to help them maximize opportunities and overcome challenges. Creating cost-effectiveness at both project implementation and operational levels is our core competency and we follow a step-by-step collaborative approach. Our team of medical practitioners, project managers, engineers and consultants is handpicked for their in-depth knowledge and experience in healthcare planning and management.

    Our clients also benefit from our team’s diverse exposure that enables them to consistently perform in multi-cultural environments. With real-world solutions that seamlessly fit each healthcare provider’s individual requirement, EHS delivers sustainable results to create meaningful and lasting value.
